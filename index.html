Our Story

A Thread That Spans Generations

Warp and Weft Creations was born from a legacy of loomwork. My grandfather, a master weaver in Khairabad, Sitapur, poured his heart into every rug he created. My father carried forward that devotion, and today, we honor their legacy by blending traditional craftsmanship with modern design. Our weavers are not just workers—they’re the soul of our company. Many of them are differently-abled artisans whose talents we proudly support through fair wages and inclusive practices. When you choose us, you're not just buying a rug—you’re investing in history, culture, and humanity.
